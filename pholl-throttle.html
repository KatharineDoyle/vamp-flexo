I came across the New York resident Pholl Throttle on what started as a casual peruse through Soundcloud and ended in my quickly searching for her contact details, as her sound seemed to fit so well with the no-shits-given, never-apologise vision of the VS series. Seamlessly tying multiple genres and moods together, this new mix expresses a passion for all sounds melancholic, melodic and trance, integrating whispy vocals and broken beats in a heavy club-ready serving. Influenced by DJ’s VTSS and brat star, as well as good friends and fellow DJ’s Arnav and Su Baykal, her mix titled ’Zeitgeber’ for VS presents a collection of tracks that build from meditative to nostalgic, into thrashing, halting, screeching and crashing. Percussion reigns supreme, expect nothing less than hefty bass and disregard for genre categorisation here.
