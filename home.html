VAMP Sonic is a platform for women, LGBTQI+ and non-gender-conforming artists to experiment and explore the boundaries of electronic music and sound art, encouraging them to go outside and beyond the box. VAMP Sonic is a space for breaking rules and putting them back together, for the wonky, alien, spooky, glamourous, euphoric 'n' transcendental, artists have the freedom to do this here. Plainly speaking, the weird shit you may not feel fit for the club, but that still has the power to make us dance. So dive in. Get bitten.
