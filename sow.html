SOW aka Sarah Fewtrell is a founder and member of the London-based collective New Scenery. The event series and recently turned label promotes Womxn, Non-binary and queer artists, promoting safe spaces at all their events welcoming acts such as Club Chai, Manara, Mike Q, Boygirl and Brat Star. Their mix for VS is a eclectic selection stretching from pop to dark underground electronic, featuring artists such as Jasper Jarvis, SOPHIE, Sega Bodega, Reelle and Aamin, testifying to the energy and boundary-breaking approach to dance music exuded at New Scenery events. It'll propel you through a multitude of emotions, showcasing SOW's gloves-off, all-frills-included selections. Heed any expectations - their crisp and refined style will break them.
