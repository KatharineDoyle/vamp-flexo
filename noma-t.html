The latest contribution by the Swiss-born, Berlin-based musician Noma-T is compiled with a spectrum of moods and personal memories, merging both found tracks and field recordings. Her mix is best summarised in this poem: - sometimes we have dreams without dreaming and when we're trying to wake up we notice we're still awake I met Tanja in the queue for a club in Berlin two years ago where we instantly connected over our mutual love of the stranger ends of electronic music and we have remained close friends ever since despite living overseas from each other. I'll never forget coming round to her flat and watching her work on a Moog modular synthesiser with such ease and understanding of its inner-workings. It was inspiring to witness her precision and her mix for VAMP showcases this; listen out for her astute ear for contrasting tempos and sounds, and a devoted love of nature in playful recordings taken from long walks through her home city's parks.
