'Touching Skin' is an ambient sound production by the multi-disciplinary creative force Mala Herba aka Zosia Ho≈Çubowska (1988, Olsztyn), created from experimentation with contact mics, never-ending feedback, and their own vocals. The non-binary do-it-yourself sound artist works during the day as a Ph.D. Fellow at the Academy of Fine Arts in Vienna, were they research queer sound art and create sound installations and performances. In the afternoon they run synthesiser workshops as a founder and curator of Sounds Queer? a queer synth laboratory. By night they transform into a curvy, lascivious witch. The artist is also a member of Oramics, a collective and agency based in Poland promoting women and queer DJs and producers. Their solo music project Mala Herba connects love for the vintage sound of synth-pop and dark-wave and creative interpretation of ethnomusicological archives. Pic by Maja Potrawiak
